## SPARK ‚Äî Brand & Visual Usage Contract

### Brand Position

SPARK is an **analyst-driven security platform** that prioritizes clarity, control, and evidence-based decision making. AI augments the analyst; it does not replace them.

---

### Visual Principles

1. **Clarity over expression**
2. **Structure over novelty**
3. **Restraint over decoration**
4. **Consistency over creativity**
5. **Timelessness over trends**

---

### Logo Usage Rules

* **Primary logo**

  * Used only for brand identity
  * Never embedded in UI workflows

* **Secondary logo**

  * Used for product representation
  * Allowed on splash screens, reports, docs

* **Tertiary icon**

  * Used everywhere in UI
  * No substitutions allowed

---

### Color Usage Rules

* Brand colors are accents, not backgrounds
* Red indicates risk, never style
* Purple indicates identity, not decoration
* Neutral tones dominate the interface

---

### Imagery Rules

**Allowed**

* Diagrams
* Structured flows
* Minimal icons
* Annotated UI screenshots

**Not allowed**

* Hacker stock photos
* Flames, sparks, lightning
* AI brains, robots, humanoids
* Masks, hoodies, skulls
* Trend-driven visuals

---

### AI Representation Rule

AI must always be shown as:

* Assistive
* Suggestive
* Reviewable
* Human-controlled

Never autonomous. Never dominant.

---

### Drift Prevention Rule

If a visual asset:

* Adds noise
* Obscures meaning
* Competes with data
* Feels trendy

It does not ship.
